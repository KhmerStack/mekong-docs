This Cookie Policy explains how MekongTunnel ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and dashboard at **mekongtunnel.dev**.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better experience. We also use browser **localStorage** and **sessionStorage** for some features.

## Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary

These cookies are required for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| `session` | Keeps you logged in after authentication | Session / 30 days (if "remember me") |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks | Session |
| `theme` | Remembers your light/dark theme preference | 1 year |
| `locale` | Remembers your language preference (en/km) | 1 year |

### Functional

These cookies improve your experience but are not strictly required.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| `dashboard_sidebar` | Remembers sidebar collapsed/expanded state | 1 year |
| `docs_last_visited` | Remembers the last docs page you visited | 30 days |

### Analytics (Optional)

We may use **privacy-focused analytics**, including **Vercel Analytics**, to understand aggregate traffic and page usage.

We do not use advertising analytics or cross-site behavioral profiling on the docs site. If our analytics setup changes in a way that affects cookies or consent requirements, we will update this policy.

## What We Do NOT Use

- No advertising or tracking cookies
- No third-party behavioral profiling cookies
- No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

## Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services used by MekongTunnel may set their own cookies:

| Service | Purpose | Their Policy |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **Cloudflare** | DDoS protection, bot detection (`__cf_bm`, `cf_clearance`) | [Cloudflare Privacy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) |
| **Stripe** | Payment processing (on `/pricing` and billing pages) | [Stripe Privacy](https://stripe.com/privacy) |
| **GitHub OAuth** | Sign-in with GitHub | [GitHub Privacy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) |
| **Google OAuth** | Sign-in with Google | [Google Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

These cookies are only set when you interact with those specific features.

## localStorage and sessionStorage

We use browser storage (not cookies) for:

- **`mekong_theme`** — active theme (dark/light)
- **`mekong_locale`** — selected language
- **`mekong_sidebar_open`** — admin/dashboard sidebar state

This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies (like `session`) will prevent you from logging in to the dashboard.
